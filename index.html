Our Story

GamerXise was created out of necessity by a founder with ADHD and a TBI survivor who was suffering from chronic migraines. Struggling to maintain healthy exercise habits, he turned to what he knew worked: video games. By merging video games and exercise, he found a way to improve his health and happiness. GamerXise is now a solution for gamers worldwide, offering full-body workouts that enhance both physical and mental well-being. Our programs are designed to improve reaction time, brain-body coordination, and gaming skills, all while you enjoy your favorite games.

Our Services
